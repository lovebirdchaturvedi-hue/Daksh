Frequently Asked Questions

Important details about your membership and our policies.

Will all contacts respond or buy? +
We do not guarantee that every buyer will respond or place an order. Business conditions change—a business could be closed, they might have temporarily stopped importing, or they may find the pricing unviable. Once your account is activated, we will align you with verified buyers, but we do not guarantee a sale.
How much profit can I expect? +
Profit margins totally depend on your negotiation skills, your product quality, and the buyer's budget. We provide the connections, but the final commercial terms are entirely between you and the buyer.
Can I upgrade my plan mid-cycle? +
Yes, absolutely. You can upgrade to a higher tier at any point during your active subscription. You only pay the difference for the remaining duration.
What is your refund policy? +
We enforce a strict No-Refund Policy. Because our service provides immediate access to digital intelligence and verified contact data, the value is transferred instantly upon account activation.
How do you verify buyers? +
Every buyer profile undergoes a strict multi-layer verification process before entering our database. We verify local domestic businesses via GSTIN, PAN, and OTP mobile checks. International buyers are vetted through Import Export Codes (IEC), verified company domains, or direct phone verification.
Is there a recurring auto-debit charge? +
No. All APD Global Trade memberships are fixed-term, one-time payments. We will never automatically charge your card or account when your plan expires. You will receive an expiration alert allowing you to manually renew.
