Institutional Verification Standards

APD Global Trade operates as a secure bridge, not a public directory. We eliminate "scam" risks by enforcing a multi-layer bank-grade vetting process for every participant in our network.

01
Buyer Vetting

Active L/C & BCL Verification

We do not accept RFQs from "unverified" sources. Every buyer must present proof of funds or an active Letter of Credit (L/C) verified by our partner banking institutions before their demand is listed.

02
Legal Audit

KYC & Entity Screening

Every account undergoes a Global Identity & Business Screening. We verify GST/VAT/EIN numbers and cross-reference against international trade watchlists to ensure legitimate commercial status.

03
Trade Integrity

Manual RFQ Scrubbing

Our trade analysts manually review every RFQ for realistic pricing, valid port specifications, and commercial feasibility. Any non-standard or suspicious inquiry is immediately purged from the system.

04
Security Layer

Encrypted Communication

Buyer contact details remain behind institutional protection. Access is only granted to Suppliers who have cleared their own internal security checks, preventing data leakage and broker interference.

Zero Tolerance for Fraud

Any attempt to submit fraudulent documentation or phishing attempts results in immediate permanent blacklisting of the entity across our global network partners.
