Institutional Role: APD Global Trade operates as a Global Trade Facilitator and Sourcing Partner. We are NOT the buyer, broker, or agent for any trade transaction. We provide the digital infrastructure, verified buyer intelligence, and networking environment to enable direct trade.

NO GUARANTEE OF OUTCOME:

APD Global Trade DOES NOT GUARANTEE confirmed sales, specific conversion rates, or a guaranteed number of buyer orders. We bring the opportunities (verified inquiries) to your desk; the execution, negotiation, and finalization of the trade depend entirely on the Supplier's competence, pricing, and product quality.

IV. TERMS OF ENGAGEMENT

1. Zero Commission & Trade Specialist Support: Our core operational motto is No Middlemen and Zero Commission. APD Global Trade charges no commission on independent trade deals closed by the Member.
